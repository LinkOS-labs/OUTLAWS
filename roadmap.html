OUTLAWS is not a corporate product. It’s a gang. Nothing here is guaranteed, everything is possible.

Launch on Monad · list on Magic Eden · get the first wave of degens through the neon door. Let the streets talk.

PFP meta, banner packs, custom assets for holders, trait-based crews and soft factions inside the collection.

Collabs with other Monad collections, shared lore, cross airdrops and whatever the ecosystem makes possible.

Phase 5 — TBD by the Gang

Holders decide where to push the story. More art? More chaos? More toys? The roadmap is a sketch — the gang is the paint.
